About Feline Friends Rescue

Founded in 2024, Feline Friends Rescue has been a haven for cats in need. Our organization is committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming cats who have been abandoned or mistreated.

Our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to provide the best care possible for these animals, ensuring they find the loving homes they deserve.

We are proud of the impact we've made in our community and look forward to continuing our mission for years to come.
